Ways that .pngs are superior to .jpgs

  • PNG allows pictures to have see-through parts, making it useful for logos and graphics with transparent backgrounds.

  • PNG is good for pictures that have clear edges, text, or drawings because it keeps the quality high and can make parts of the image see-through .

  • PNG supports a wide range of colours, able to support images that require different colours.

Ways that .pngs are inferior to .jpgs

  • PNG files are not the best choice for storing photos on the internet because they are usually bigger in size compared to the same pictures saved in JPG format.

  • With photos, it's better to use JPG because it can make the file size smaller without losing too much quality, thanks to its special compression technique.
